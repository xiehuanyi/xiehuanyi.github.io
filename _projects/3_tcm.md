**Principal Investigator** (Nov 2022 - Jan 2023)

Applied large-scale pre-trained models to traditional Chinese medicine diagnosis.
- **Model**: Used Ernie-3.0-zh series of models to classify 148 different Chinese medicine syndrome types.
- **Experiments**: Designed a series of experiments to verify the impact of different model sizes and different model inputs on model performance.
